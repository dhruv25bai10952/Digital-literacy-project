## 📌 Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created a one-page visual infographic using **Canva** covering the definition of digital literacy, essential digital tools for students, and safe internet practices. The infographic is designed to help batchmates quickly grasp the importance of being digitally literate in today's world.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle** to establish a strong digital presence. Each platform serves a specific career and academic purpose, forming the foundation of a four-year professional development plan.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner coding challenge on **HackerRank** and created a five-question **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using Google Forms, demonstrating skills in both technical practice and online collaboration.

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Professional Communication
Drafted two professional emails (deadline extension request and internship application) and created a comprehensive Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist for college students, reflecting best practices in digital communication.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched **UPI/Online Payment Fraud** and produced a detailed case study along with an eight-point 'Stay Safe Online' prevention checklist tailored to college students in India, including financial safety tips and reporting channels.
